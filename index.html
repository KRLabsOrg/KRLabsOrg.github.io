About Us

KR Labs develops transparent and trustworthy AI solutions for natural language processing (NLP) tasks. KR Labs was founded by NLP researchers Ádám Kovács and Gábor Recski. Our technology is based on algorithms first described in Kovács et al. 2022 and Recski et al. 2018. Between 2023 and 2024, KR Labs was incubated in the TU Wien i²ncubator at the TU Wien Innovation Incubation Center (i²c).
